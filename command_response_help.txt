Start Tracking
To register a product for tracking
/track ProductAsin ProductUrl

Manual Updates
To receive a manual update for a single product, reply to any of the said product related messages sent by the bot with the "/update" command.
To receive a manual update for all of the registered products
/updateall

Stop Updates
To stop receiving updates for a product, reply to any of the said product related messages sent by the bot with the "/stop" command.
To stop receiving updates for all of your registered products
/stopall

Restart Updates
To restart updates for a product you previously stopped, reply to any of the said product related messages sent by the bot with the "/restart" command.
